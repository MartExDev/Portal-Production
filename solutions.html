ShopFi opens ShopFi new ecology

ShopFi is committed to creating a ShopFi transaction and shopping platform combining cryptocurrency and global traditional e-commerce, providing a strong driving force for blockchain. Our ultimate goal is to provide infrastructure for blockchain, create value for blockchain ecology, better solve the problem of transaction settlement, and create a new model and new world of mutual trust, security, openness and sharing.

Decentralized e-commerce

ShopFi is a decentralized e-commerce trading platform, which provides users with a bridge for transactions between major global traditional e-commerce platforms. Users can freely use cryptocurrency to purchase needed commodities from major e-commerce platforms (e.g. House, car, furniture, electronics, daily necessities, cosmetics, luxury goods, etc.), so that users can use cryptocurrency to buy any goods around the world.

Network Roles

In ShopFi's product line, there are three main user roles:

① Consumers: Crypto holders
② Brokers: Who are responsible to deal the order for customer-made.
③ Sellers: Traditional e-commerce platforms.

Privacy Preserving

When users place an order, public information is stored in the blockchain storage (IPFS, Arweave, etc.), the key information and storage hash are submitted to the smart contract. The encrypted private information (such as receiving information, etc.) is transmitted to the corresponding allocated node through SSL tunnel using P2P(Peer to Peer) technology. After receiving information, only specific node can decrypt it with its own private key, together with public data from smart contract and blockchain storage to form a complete order. After the order is finished, all the sensitive information will be removed from node itself.
