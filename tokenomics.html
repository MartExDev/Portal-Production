$ShopFi is a multichain ERC20-compatible token
The Token that empowers ShopFi's Economy
$ShopFi is ShopFi's main token, it plays a vital role in ShopFi's ecological ecosystem. With $ShopFi's support, ShopFi ecosystem is able to build trust between roles and components using special mechanism. It empowers $ShopFi holder different rights and interests as well as rightful authority in ShopFi DAO.
What is the Function of $ShopFi in ShopFi?
In ShopFi ecology, $ShopFi plays an extremely important role in all aspects.
Ecosystem Managing
ShopFi aims to be a permissionless and leaderless decentralized ecosystem. By pledging $ShopFi, ShopFi keeps a balanced environment between different roles.
Network Protection
To be able to protect the entire network, Brokers and Nodes are required to pledge $ShopFi to be able to do the their work. They will be rewarded if the jobs are done right or else be punished if they run against the intrests of the system.
Online Payments
You can pay directly with $ShopFi for products and services from specific merchants and get discounts by holding a certain amount of $ShopFi tokens.
Fee deduction
Directly using your $ShopFi to get lower rates of fees if you are a $ShopFi holder for specific merchants on ShopFi. There will be more to come.
